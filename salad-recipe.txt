Ingredients:
 - Cabbage
 - Carrots
 - Broccoli
 - raisins, craisins, or similar
 - sunflower seeds
 - baked potatoes
 - lemon
 - olive oil
 - shallots

Directions:
 - chop shallot into small pieces and add to bowl with 1/2 squeezed lemon juice
 - shred Cabbage and add 1/2 lemon to cabbage and massage with hands
 - shred Carrots, Broccoli, and mix in a bowl with raisins, sunflower seeds, baked potatoes, etc.
 - after the lemon juice has cooked the shallots, add olive oil similar volume to lemon juice
 - cover, stir, and enjoy!
